AURE

The freshness of a subtle and charming effervescence, the elegance of a seductive fruitiness.

A blend chosen for its creamy freshness, dynamism, and complexity. It is a Champagne of sharing, intended to bring together as many people as possible.

GRAPE VARIETIES

Approx. 45% Chardonnay, 45% Pinot Noir, 10% Meunier, including 1/3 of reserve wines.

AGING

8 months in stainless steel tanks to maintain purity.

WINEMAKING

Traditional, low-interventionist.

BOTTLE AGING

2 to 3 years.

DOSAGE

Extra Brut with perpetual reserve, which gives it roundness and gourmandise.

TECHNICAL SHEET

Energy 332Kcal/ 80kj Fat 0g (saturated 0g) Carbohydrates 1.2g (sugars <0.5g) Protein 0g Salt 0g

This harmonious cuvée is composed of approximately 45% Pinot Noir, 45% Chardonnay, and 10% Meunier. This blend always includes a significant portion of reserve wine (up to 5 years), around 35%.

TASTING

The dress is a brilliant golden yellow, animated by a lively and fine effervescence. The nose provides intense aromas of brioche and white fruits. In the mouth, we find white-fleshed fruits meeting pastries; the delicate bubble offers a creamy and seductive touch.

PAIRINGS

A shareable aperitif wine. Perfect pairings: Aperitifs with gougères, cold meats, and hard cheeses like Comté or simple dishes like roasted poultry.
